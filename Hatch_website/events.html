Workshops & Events

Hatch offers hands-on workshops on topics ranging from embroidery to laser cutting. Anything you make at Hatch, you can take home with you (with a few notable exceptions). We love projects that give you a tangible outcome that is entirely yours! Hatch also hosts several special events every year. You can register for upcoming workshops and events online or by calling Hatch during open hours: 781-795-2309.
